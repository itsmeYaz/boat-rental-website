About Us

Embark on a seafaring escapade with GasparGem, where the heart of our boat rental services lies in the skilled hands of local fishermen. Born from the rich maritime heritage of our coastal community, GasparGem offers a unique opportunity to cruise the waters in vessels owned and operated by those who intimately know the sea. Experience the authenticity of a nautical adventure with us, where each boat is not just a rental but a voyage guided by the expertise and passion of our local seafaring hosts.
